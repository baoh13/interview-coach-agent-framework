**About the job**

Job Title: Technical Lead

Contract: 12 months

Inside IR

Technet has partnered with a long-established, Microsoft-aligned software business in
the legal sector to appoint a Contract Delivery Lead / Technical Authority for a live AI-
first product programme.

The business operates a 35-year-old, feature-rich .NET practice management platform
(Azure hosted) and is now building a focused, AI-first product extension that integrates
back into the core system. Following a challenging prior engagement, they require a
strong delivery reset.

This is not a Scrum Master or reporting PM role.

We are seeking a hands-on Technical Delivery Lead who will:

- Take full ownership of sprint structure, technical Jira ticketing and delivery
    cadence
- Reset scope, sequencing and commercial focus
- Lead and galvanise a small engineering team
- Provide clear, defensible reporting on risks, timelines and impact
- Act as the technical bridge between developers and senior stakeholders
- Champion AI-first development (AI-assisted coding, QA automation, and
    practical AI integration into the product)

You will bring:

- Proven experience stabilising or rescuing live software programmes


- Strong Microsoft stack exposure (Azure, .NET environment preferred)
- Backend depth with broad architectural awareness
- Experience shaping CI/CD pipelines and delivery hygiene
- Commercial judgement – ability to challenge feature creep and protect release
    integrity
- Confident stakeholder management, including technically inquisitive founders

This is a small, fast-moving environment with quick decision-making and strong
executive engagement. The right individual will operate as the technical authority and
delivery owner, not just a coordinator.

If you are comfortable taking control of a live programme, enforcing clarity, and
delivering a commercially viable AI-enabled product, we would welcome a confidential
conversation.
